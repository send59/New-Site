How often should I check my fire extinguisher?

At least once a month, ensure it's charged, undamaged and accessible.

Can I use water on all types of types of fires?

No, never use water on electrical or oil fires; use the correct extinguisher type instead.

How can I reduce fire risk at home?

Keep flammable items away from heat, avoid overloading sockets and check appliances regurlarly.

What does fire safety compliance mean?

It means meeting all legal and regulatory requirements for preventing, detecting and responding to fires in your premises.

What happens if i'm not compliant?

You risk fines, legal action, business closure and increased danger to lives and property.

How often should compliance checks be done?

At least annualy, plus regular in-house safety inspections.
